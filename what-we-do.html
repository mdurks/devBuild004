Assessing your pension is simple.

Once we have received the pension information from your providers and our research is complete, we'll invite you to book a call with one of our expert pension advisers.

They will inform you what we've found out and how your pensions compare against the best in the market. You will also receive a suitability report tailored to you individually.

What's covered in your assessment

  • How much you currently pay out in charges and admin fees.
  • Your pension valuations, performance and projected income at retirement.
  • Ensure the investment risk level is appropriate for you.
  • Options to protect your money from economic downturns.
  • Money saving solutions for multiple pensions.
  • Eligibility for any pension benefits.
  • More tax efficient pension plan options.
  • Better suited pensions if available.
  • Flexibility of current pension arrangements.
  • Your options from age 55 and at retirement.
